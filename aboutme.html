About me

I am currently attending Epicodus, a dev bootcamp in Portland, OR, to hone my software development skills.

Prior to that, I had been working in the Government Technology sector for 12 years. During that time I’ve served in multiple roles, which has allowed me to gain a thorough understanding of both customer, internal team, and business needs. Channeling this knowledge has enabled my success as a Product Manager for the last 10 years. Product Management can be a big umbrella covering many different business needs and I’ve done them all: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Trainer, Sales Engineer, User Researcher, Market Analyst, and some Software Engineering.

When I’m not at work, you can find me at the ice rink for hockey, outside for a variety of activities like snowboarding, water skiing, camping and hiking, or at an event space for live music and comedy.
